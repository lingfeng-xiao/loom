## Maintenance Rules

- Every abnormal behavior, workaround, and engineering debt item should be recorded in [Known Issues & Fix Backlog](./known-issues.md).
- Keep the backlog current with the latest verified status, priority, impact, and fix strategy.
- When an item is fixed, move it to the resolved section instead of deleting the history unless the team agrees to archive it.
